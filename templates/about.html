Clear Mind

The Clear Mind website is designed to support individuals with short-term memory loss and hearing impairments by providing an intuitive platform that leverages advanced large language models (LLMs). The website allows users to record conversations or important audio notes and automatically link these recordings to specific individuals. These recordings are then stored in a user-friendly calendar system, enabling easy retrieval and review at any time. This solution aims to empower users by helping them manage their day-to-day interactions and memories more effectively, thus improving their overall quality of life.
